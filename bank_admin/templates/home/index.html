CwebPay is a bank management system with 4 different admin interface which are

  1. Bank Admin
  2. Teller Admins
  3. Front Desk Admins
  4. Customers Admin

Bank Admin has the responsibility to manage the Tellers which are to:

  1. Register Tellers
  2. Fund Teller acount
  3. View all Transaction History and by each Teller
  4. Delete or Block Teller

Teller Admins deals with transactions only which are to:

  1. Receive Cash from Customers and credit their account while debiting his or her own account
  2. Pay Customers and debit their account while crediting his or her own teller account
  3. All withdrawal charges are credited to bank admin who fund Tellers account

Front Desk Admins has deals with the bank customer affairs which are to:

  1. Register Customer for physical dealings or
  2. Update their account with provided KYC documents
  3. View customer Transaction history for any customer complain
  4. Delete or Block a customer

Customer Admins area where they:

  1. Transfer between customers
  2. View their transaction history
  3. Upload KYC documents

Developed With:

  1. Python
  2. Django
  3. MYSQL
